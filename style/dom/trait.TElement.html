fn deprecated_dirty_bit_is_set(&self) -> bool

The concept of a dirty bit doesn't exist in our new restyle algorithm. Instead, we associate restyle and change hints with nodes. However, we continue to allow the dirty bit to trigger unconditional restyles while we transition both Servo and Stylo to the new architecture.
