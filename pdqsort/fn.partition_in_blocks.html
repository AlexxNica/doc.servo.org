fn partition_in_blocks<T, F>(v: &mut [T], pivot: &T, is_less: &mut F) -> usize where F: FnMut(&T, &T) -> bool

Partitions v into elements smaller than pivot, followed by elements greater than or equal to pivot. Returns the number of elements smaller than pivot.

Partitioning is performed block-by-block in order to minimize the cost of branching operations. This idea is presented in the BlockQuicksort paper.
