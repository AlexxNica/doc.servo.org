struct Frame {
    id: FrameId,
    prev: Vec<FrameState>,
    current: FrameState,
    next: Vec<FrameState>,
}

A frame in the frame tree. Each frame is the constrellation's view of a browsing context. Each browsing context has a session history, caused by navigation and traversing the history. Each frame has its current entry, plus past and future entries. The past is sorted chronologically, the future is sorted reverse chronoogically: in partiucular prev.pop() is the latest past entry, and next.pop() is the earliest future entry.
